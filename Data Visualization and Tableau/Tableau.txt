Types of Variables: 

- Dimensions- Categorical Variable - 
qualitative values in tableau (such as names, dates, or geographical data) are called Dimensions. 
We will use dimensions to categorize, segment, and reveal the details in our data. Dimensions affect the level of detail in the view. 

- Measures- Quantitative Variable - numeric, 
quantitative values in tableau (values that you can measure) called Measures, 
Measures can be aggregated (group by). 

Dimensions and Measures can be Discrete or Continuous. 


The Viz: 
The basic structure of every viz is made of, 
dimension and measure columns placed on the Rows and Columns shelves, 
and on different properties in the Marks card.
