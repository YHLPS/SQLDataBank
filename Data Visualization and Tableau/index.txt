Dashboards: 
Enables analysis, monitoring, measurement and comparison of data.

Every dashboard will be created for a certain purpose.
The data displayed and the graphs displayed will be chosen according to the purpose of the dashboard.
The design and layout of the dashboard should also match the purpose of the dashboard.

Main Dashboard- will contain links and summery to all the dashboards.

Design Rules- As with any visualization, there are design rules that will help make the dashboard more understandable, and will convey the message better:
- Text - size, color, bold
- Color
- Frame
- Position & Hierarchy

Interactivity - Customers can be allowed to perform actions that will affect the display of data on the dashboard:
- ToolTip
- Filter
- Zoom
